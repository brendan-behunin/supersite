Like the Guardians of the Galaxy, the Avengers have had many changes to which characters are on the team. As of the last installment, the members of the avengers include Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Blackwidow, Hawkeye, Falcon, Vision, the Scarlet Witch, War Machine, the Winter Soldier, Spiderman and Antman.

The Avengers are also known as Earth's Mightiest heroes.
